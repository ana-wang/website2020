About

x

Ana Wang is a content creator focused on culture, learning and technology.

She was previously at Shopify and now works at education startup SuperHi while running her independent and intermittently available project studio, P/ET/AL, where she focuses on bringing playfulness using color, mood and culture to topics that explore transformation and perception on the internet. Trained as a fashion designer, Ana works to highlight and celebrate contrast: between soft and hard, art and technology, optimism and reality, the engineered and the imagined, the deep long form and tiny wonderful things.

Her independent projects have been featured in Vogue, Forbes and the Huffington Post. She has also been a Top Writer on Medium in the love and relationships category as well as been featured on the Shopify blog, the Evernote blog and the world's biggest trend forecasting network on topics as diverse as fashion trends, note-taking and customer experience.

Ana currently teaches all kinds of things, from beginners how to code to how to build a creative career. She's most interested in teaching people how to challenge their own perceptions of the world and themselves.

In 2020, Ana started a la modem, a column exploring creative influence.
